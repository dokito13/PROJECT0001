THE WORLD OF STAR WARS



                   It is a period of civil war. Rebel spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against evil Galactic Empire.

                   During the battle, Rebel spies managed to steal secret plans to the Empire's ultimate weapon, the DEATH STAR, and space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet.

                   Pursued by the Empire's sinister agent, Princess Leia races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her people and restore freedom to the galaxy.

LOG INTO STAR WARS WORLD







Star Wars: Episode VII- The Force Awakens

The story begins with the disappearance of Luke Skywalker, the last Jedi. The First Order, led by Kylo Ren, rises to power and seeks to destroy Skywalker. General Leia Organa leads the Resistance in the fight against the First Order. She sends her pilot, Poe Dameron, to Jakku to find a clue to Luke's whereabouts.

On Jakku, Poe obtains a map to Luke and hides it in a droid named BB-8. He is captured by the First Order, but Finn, a disillusioned stormtrooper, helps him escape. Finn encounters Rey, a scavenger on Jakku, and together they escape the First Order's pursuit aboard the Millennium Falcon.

They seek help from Maz Kanata and eventually join the Resistance. Rey is captured by Kylo Ren, who tries to extract information from her mind. Rey discovers her own Force abilities and escapes. The Resistance plans an attack on the Starkiller Base, a planet-destroying weapon. Han Solo confronts his son, Kylo Ren, but is killed in the process.

The Resistance, with the help of Finn and Rey, launches an assault on the Starkiller Base. Rey faces Kylo Ren in a lightsaber duel and emerges victorious. The Resistance destroys the base, and Rey, Chewbacca, and R2-D2 set off to find Luke Skywalker. The story ends with Rey offering Luke his old lightsaber.
